# 🛡️ Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected on my personal website [dossantosee.eng.br](https://dossantos.eng.br/). This policy is designed to comply with global data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR - Europe), the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD - Brazil), and the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA - United States).

---

## 📥 Part 1: Information Collected and Purpose

> [!NOTE]
> **LIMITED DATA COLLECTION.**  
> I collect this information for the sole purpose of reading and responding to your inquiries. I do not use this information for marketing purposes, and I do not add you to any mailing lists without a separate, explicit request.

### 1. Information I Collect
When you use the contact form on my website, I collect the following Personal Identifiable Information (PII) that you voluntarily provide:
1. **Name**
2. **Email address**
3. **The content of your message**

## 🌐 Part 2: Third-Party Services and Data Processing

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **DATA PROCESSOR ACKNOWLEDGMENT.**  
> My website is a static, personal site. To process the contact form, I use a third-party service called **FormSubmit** (formsubmit.co). 

When you submit a message, your data is sent via FormSubmit's servers before being forwarded to my personal email inbox. By using the contact form, you acknowledge that your information will be processed by FormSubmit. 
*   You can review FormSubmit's Privacy Policy here: [https://formsubmit.co/privacy](https://formsubmit.co/privacy)

Because FormSubmit and my email provider may use servers located in the United States or other countries, your data may be transferred internationally.

---

## 🔐 Part 3: Data Security and Retention

> [!CAUTION]
> **STRICT CONFIDENTIALITY.**  
> I treat your communication strictly as personal correspondence. Once your email reaches my inbox, it is protected by the security measures of my email provider. I do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to anyone.

### Data Retention
I will keep your contact information and messages only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected (e.g., to resolve your inquiry or maintain a conversational thread). Afterward, the emails will be routinely deleted.

## ⚖️ Part 4: Your Global Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live (such as the EU, Brazil, or California), you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

*   **(a) Right to Access:** You can ask me what personal data I hold about you.
*   **(b) Right to Rectification:** You can ask me to correct any inaccurate data.
*   **(c) Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can request that I delete your previous emails.
*   **(d) Right to Withdraw Consent:** You can withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me directly via the contact form on my website. I will respond to your request within 30 days.
